Glossary

o Ija- word for mother in Uttarakhand’s Language


o Daju – word for elder brother in Uttarakhand’s Language


o Barbajyu- word for paternal grandfather in Uttarakhand’s Language


o Dubke- dish made in Uttarakhand; simple dal paste made with tuvar dal and the pakoras/dumplings are also made with the same dal.


o Bodi- word for father’s elder brother’s wife in Uttarakhand’s Language


o Chchi- word for father’s younger brother’s wife in Uttarakhand’s Language


o Chcha- word for father’s younger brother in Uttarakhand’s Language


o Bal mithai- sweet dish made in Uttarakhand


o Arsa- sweet dish made in Uttarakhand


o Jhangori kheer- sweet dish made in Uttarakhand


o Kafuli- a delicacy of Uttarakhand prepared out of leafy greens such as palak (spinach) and methi (fenugreek) leaves.
