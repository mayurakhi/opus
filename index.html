Badri Dutt Pandey

On 14th January 1921, people from different villages gathering a crowd of nearly 40 thousand moved to the confluence of sarayu and gomati raising flags and banner with ‘END COOLIE-BEGAR’ inscribed on them under the leadership of BADRI DUTT PANDEY.For the success of the movement Badri Dutt Pandey came to be known as ‘KUMAUN KESARI’- the lion of Kumaun. »

Barrister Mukundi Lal

April 13,1930 thousand of people had gathered at Kissa Khani market in front of the polic station in Peshawar in protest of the British rule and demand for independence. On this the British captain ordered the garhwal rifles to open fire at the crowd as soon as the order was shouted much to the surprise veer Chandra Singh loudly ordered the troupe to cease fire on questioning by the superiors, he said that he won’t be firing at innocent unarmed people..Mukundi Lal ji went to Abetabad to fight the case of thirty-nine garhwal rifles known for his intelligent litigation he made strong points before judge in support of actions taken by soldiers consequently judge is convinced y barrister Mukundi Lal ji’s oral argument. And hence they were barred from death penalty. »

Mohan Joshi

Mohan Joshi has been a name in the freedom struggle of Kumaun, which has made a special place not only as a fighter, but also as a successful editor. Mohan Joshi was such a successful fighter whose intellectuals were also convinced of his scholarship. There was a quality of cowardice and inertia in his speeches. When he gave Vyravyan, he would have made the holy Ganges of Swarajya flow. His writings would ignite in the newspaper and create life in the dead hearts, such a soulful writing style is rare in Hindi journalism even today. »

They are revolutionary who used non-violence as the weapon proving that pen is mightier than the sword.
